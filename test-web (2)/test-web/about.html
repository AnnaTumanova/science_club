Who we are?

The ML Club serves as a place of community for discussing, learning about, and working on topics related to machine learning and artificial intelligence across disciplines. Meeting activities include discussions of current events in AI, professor talks, tutorials, and workshops.

The main goal of this club is to allow people to develop and enhance their skills which would help them to understand the principles of ML, data science and data analysis. This club is mainly for students who would like to develop skills and can share their own industry experience and thoughts.

Also now we are progressing on developing a website where all the participants could ask technical/non-technical questions. In this club, after getting a better understanding of the basic tools we will continue to develop and apply machine learning to the areas which could be improved (health system, social issues, education and so on).
